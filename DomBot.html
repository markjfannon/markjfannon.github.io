DomBot

DomBot is a Discord bot made in JS, modelled off Dominic Howard of the band "Muse".
He was made for a Muse fan server, with several features to fit it's, uh, strangenature. Several features include responses tagging other bots, some of which may not be present on your own server.
The main feauture is random face emoji responses to messages that begin with "DomBot" that are not commands. His command prefix is "DomBot", and has a "help" command to list his commands and how to use them. Add DomBot to your server here!
